Clearance Data Disclaimer

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Read this before using any clearance information from this site to make driving decisions. The data on Willifit is provided for general reference only. The posted clearance at the entrance of any garage, tunnel, or bridge is the only authoritative figure — always verify in person before committing your vehicle.

Data is not guaranteed accurate

Willifit aggregates clearance data from a mix of sources: operator websites, community reports, OpenStreetMap, first-hand measurements, and published specifications. We do our best to keep it current and accurate, but:

  • Clearances change when buildings are renovated, re-signed, or resurfaced
  • Posted signs sometimes differ from actual clearance
  • Clearance may vary between levels, entrances, or lanes of the same structure
  • Temporary obstructions (construction, decorations, snow buildup) can reduce usable clearance
  • Some entries are marked "Unverified" or "Needs verification" — these are our best guesses, not confirmed measurements

Recommended safety margin

Industry convention for commercial drivers is at least 6 inches between your vehicle height and the posted clearance. We use that same threshold when showing "Tight" warnings in the app. Smaller margins are risky — sign sag, pavement variation, and load shifts can consume the buffer.

Report errors

If you find incorrect data, please use the "Report clearance" button on any garage detail page. Confirmed reports improve the dataset for everyone. Thank you.
