Frequently asked

What's the lowest-clearance parking garage in San Francisco, California?
The lowest verified clearance in San Francisco is 6'8" (80 inches) at SFMOMA — Minna St Entrance. Standard cars and small SUVs fit, but vans, RVs, and box trucks should look elsewhere.
What's the highest-clearance parking option in San Francisco?
SFMOMA — Mission St Entrance has the tallest verified clearance in San Francisco at 7'2" (86 inches), which accommodates most box trucks and smaller RVs.
Which parking facilities in San Francisco accept RVs or oversized vehicles?
22 facilities in San Francisco are explicitly marked as oversized-vehicle-friendly: Ferry Building Surface Lot (Pier 14 area), 5th & Brannan Surface Lot, Embarcadero/Bryant Surface Lot and 19 more. See the full list on the interactive map at willifit.ai/#san-francisco-ca.
Are there RV parks in San Francisco?
Yes. 4 RV park(s) are indexed in San Francisco, including Candlestick RV Park, Treasure Island RV Park, San Francisco RV Resort and 1 more. RV parks have no overhead clearance — any vehicle size fits.
How is the clearance data verified?
Each posted clearance is read directly from the sign at the garage entrance using Google Street View imagery and Claude Vision (Anthropic's image AI). Only readings with high confidence are committed; we record the exact Street View pano ID and the verification date so anyone can independently confirm in Google Maps.
