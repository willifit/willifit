Frequently asked

What's the lowest-clearance parking garage in Sacramento, California?
The lowest verified clearance in Sacramento is 6'9" (81 inches) at Golden 1 Center — DOCO Garage. Standard cars and small SUVs fit, but vans, RVs, and box trucks should look elsewhere.
What's the highest-clearance parking option in Sacramento?
SMF — Terminal A Garage has the tallest verified clearance in Sacramento at 8'2" (98 inches), which accommodates most box trucks and smaller RVs.
Which parking facilities in Sacramento accept RVs or oversized vehicles?
20 facilities in Sacramento are explicitly marked as oversized-vehicle-friendly: Old Sacramento Historic District Lot, William Land Park / Zoo Lots, California State Railroad Museum Lot and 17 more. See the full list on the interactive map at willifit.ai/#sacramento-ca.
Are there RV parks in Sacramento?
Yes. 4 RV park(s) are indexed in Sacramento, including Sacramento Sun RV Park, SacWest RV Park & Campground, Cal Expo RV Park and 1 more. RV parks have no overhead clearance — any vehicle size fits.
How is the clearance data verified?
4 of the 125 locations on this page were verified against a published source such as the facility's own website or operator listing, with the verification date recorded on each entry. The remaining 121 are imported from OpenStreetMap and the U.S. National Bridge Inventory and are not individually verified. Always confirm at the posted sign before you drive.
