What's the lowest-clearance parking garage in Boston, Massachusetts?
The lowest verified clearance in Boston is 5'8" (68 inches) at Back Bay Hilton / Prudential Center Garage. Standard cars and small SUVs fit, but vans, RVs, and box trucks should look elsewhere.
What's the highest-clearance parking option in Boston?
Logan Airport — Central Garage has the tallest verified clearance in Boston at 8'2" (98 inches), which accommodates most box trucks and smaller RVs.
Which parking facilities in Boston accept RVs or oversized vehicles?
