What's the lowest-clearance parking garage in San Antonio, Texas?
The lowest verified clearance in San Antonio is 6'0" (72 inches) at Hyatt Regency San Antonio Riverwalk Garage. Standard cars and small SUVs fit, but vans, RVs, and box trucks should look elsewhere.
What's the highest-clearance parking option in San Antonio?
SAPark — Navarro Garage has the tallest verified clearance in San Antonio at 11'6" (138 inches), which accommodates most box trucks and smaller RVs.
Which parking facilities in San Antonio accept RVs or oversized vehicles?

Are there RV parks in San Antonio?
Yes. 11 RV park(s) are indexed in San Antonio, including RV Park (unnamed), KOA Kampground, Cozy Cove RV Park and 8 more. RV parks have no overhead clearance — any vehicle size fits.
How is the clearance data verified?
8 of the 53 locations on this page are AI-verified: the posted clearance was read directly from the entrance sign in Google Street View using Claude Vision (Anthropic's image AI), and we store the exact Street View pano so you can open it and check the sign yourself. The remaining 45 are imported from OpenStreetMap and the U.S. National Bridge Inventory and are not individually verified. Always confirm at the posted sign before you drive.
