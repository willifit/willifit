Frequently asked

What's the lowest-clearance parking garage in Spokane, Washington?
The lowest verified clearance in Spokane is 6'6" (78 inches) at River Park Square Garage. Standard cars and small SUVs fit, but vans, RVs, and box trucks should look elsewhere.
What's the highest-clearance parking option in Spokane?
Spokane GEG — Daily Garage has the tallest verified clearance in Spokane at 8'6" (102 inches), which accommodates most box trucks and smaller RVs.
Which parking facilities in Spokane accept RVs or oversized vehicles?
14 facilities in Spokane are explicitly marked as oversized-vehicle-friendly: Riverfront Park / Expo Lot, Avista Stadium (Spokane Indians), Spokane County Fair & Expo Center and 11 more. See the full list on the interactive map at willifit.ai/#spokane-wa.
Are there RV parks in Spokane?
Yes. 5 RV park(s) are indexed in Spokane, including Park Lane RV Park, Trailer Inns RV Park, North Spokane RV Park and 2 more. RV parks have no overhead clearance — any vehicle size fits.
How is the clearance data verified?
