Who the audience is

Every user on WillIFit is solving a specific problem: "will my vehicle fit?" They typically:

  • Drive a box truck, moving van, RV, cargo van, tow truck, or commercial rig
  • Are mid-trip or mid-move — they're about to spend money on parking, fuel, a tow, an insurance claim, or a windshield
  • Are looking at a specific city, often a city they don't live in, often within the next few hours
  • Have already told us their vehicle height, so we know who to show what

This is the most intent-filtered audience you will ever see for your spend. No guessing at demographics — if a user is on our site, they are your customer.
